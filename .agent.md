## §7 Common mistakes to avoid

- ❌ Missing `Expected output:` blocks under CODE.md snippets
- ❌ Missing `# Expected output:` comments in code.py `__main__`
- ❌ Bare `except:` instead of specific exception types
- ❌ Vague error messages (`"invalid"` instead of `f"must be 0..5, got {x}"`)
- ❌ Dead code (`raise NotImplementedError` after `return`)
- ❌ Using `list` for membership checks instead of `set` (O(n²) vs O(n))
- ❌ Fewer than 6 snippets in Python CODE.md or 5 in DSA CODE.md
- ❌ DSA CODE.md without Visual/Diagram section or traversal blocks
- ❌ Skipping subtopics listed in `DAILY_STUDY_PLAN.md`
